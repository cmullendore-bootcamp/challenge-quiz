# Javascript Quiz
This javascript quiz presents a series of questions that may test
your knowledge of Javascript as well as several other generalized
and fun topics. Enjoy!

## Rules
- When you click Start you will have 60 seconds to complete the quiz.
- You receive questions for any questions you answer correctly, and do not receive credit for any question answered incorrectly or not at all (such as when time expires).
- When the time expires you the test is ended and you will lose credit for any unanswered questions.
- After you complete the quiz you will be prompted to save your score. 
- To view all past scores, click the "High Scores" button on the upper-left of the screen.
